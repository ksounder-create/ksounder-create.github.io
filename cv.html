Curriculum Vitae — Soundar Krish Soundar Krish, Ph.D. Independent Scientist Founder, RuleBasedBiology.org soundar@rulebasedbiology.org Summary Dr. Soundar Krish is a biochemist and molecular biology researcher with more than two decades of academic and medical research experience in India, the United States, and Saudi Arabia. His work has included NIH-funded projects in cancer biology and pharmacogenetics, and long-term faculty and research appointments in leading biomedical institutions. This discovery resulted from analyses of publicly available experimental data accumulated over several decades and represents a foundational shift in molecular biology, medicine, and biotechnology.
